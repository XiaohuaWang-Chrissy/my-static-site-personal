La Forma del Diamante

The Shape of a Diamond

Date April 6, 2025
Location Buenos Aires, Argentina
Directed by Chrissy WangJing Chen

In a country where inflation runs faster than time, Marisol Fernández is trying to rebuild herself. She crossed borders for love, and returned home with scars.

This story "La Forma del Diamante" is about work, survival, and self-worth;

About shining, even when it hurts.
